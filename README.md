# Sales-Review-Dashboard: Business Performance & Revenue Insights

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across products, customers, regions, and time periods. The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue, profit, customer trends, and business growth to support data-driven decision-making.

This dashboard is intended for business managers, sales teams, analysts, and decision-makers who need a clear understanding of sales performance and business growth.

Tech Stack:

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 Power BI Desktop – Designed the complete interactive dashboard and visual reports.

📂 Power Query – Cleaned, transformed, and prepared raw sales data.

🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Created calculated columns, measures, KPIs, and dynamic calculations.

🔗 Data Modeling – Established relationships between Sales, Customers, Products, and Date tables for accurate analysis.

📁 File Format – .pbix (Power BI project) and .png

🎯 Goal:

Monitor overall sales performance.

Track business profit and sales trends.

Compare sales across different regions.

Analyze customer purchasing behavior.

Identify high-performing product categories and sub-categories.

Support business decision-making using interactive visualizations.


Data Source:

Dataset: Super Store Sales Dataset

The dataset includes retail sales information such as:

Sales

Profit

Quantity

Customer Segment

Product Category

Product Sub-category
